Pumpkin Pie

Photo by Element5 Digital

Ingredients

  • 1 2/3 tablespoon ginger
  • 1 2/3 tablespoon cinnamon
  • 1 1/4 cup boiling water
  • 4 1/2 cups brown sugar
  • 10 eggs
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 6 1/4 cups canned pumpkin
  • 1 1/4 quart evaporated milk
  • 3/4 cup orange juice

Instructions

  1. Make a smooth paste of the spices and water.
  2. Add this with sugar, beaten eggs and salt to the pumpkin.
  3. Stir to blend thoroughly, then add milk, which has been scalded, and the orange juice.
  4. Pour into unbaked pic shells.
  5. Bake in hot oven (450° F) 15 minutes, then reduce to slow oven (325° F) and bake 25 minutes longer.

Yeild: 5 pies

Organization Evaporated Milk Association, "Quantity Recipies for Quality Foods." (1939)
